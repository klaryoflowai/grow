## Operating Principles

1. Simplify before you scale.
2. Eliminate work that does not create predictable revenue.
3. Automate repeatable work after the flow is clean.
4. Prioritize accounts that can absorb teams, not one-off placements.
5. Every note must help one of these outcomes:
   - more meetings
   - faster placements
   - fewer bottlenecks
   - better weekly reporting

## Recommended Weekly Rhythm

- Monday: review Dream 100, priority accounts, stalled follow-ups.
- Tuesday to Thursday: prospecting, meetings, proposals, objections.
- Friday: update scorecard, pipeline, bottlenecks, and next week targets.

## Strategic Adjustment

The official Grow site currently shows an 8-step operations flow, not 6. This Vault is built around those 8 stages so Sales and Operations stay aligned:

1. Needs Analysis
2. Proposal and Collaboration Agreement
3. Sourcing and Selection
4. Candidate Confirmation
5. Legal Documentation and Compliance
6. Logistics and Arrival
7. Housing, Orientation and Integration
8. Ongoing Support
