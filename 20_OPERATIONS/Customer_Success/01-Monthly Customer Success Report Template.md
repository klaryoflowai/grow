# Monthly Customer Success Report Template

## Purpose

This note captures the operating structure for Grow's monthly customer success report.

Delivery rule: send the report to the client by the 5th day of the following month.

## Report Structure

### 1. Report Identification

Track:

- client company
- reporting month
- contract number
- effective date
- current month of contract
- total contracted workers
- active workers in the current month
- Grow project manager
- report issue date

### 2. The 8 Customer Success KPIs

#### KPI 1. Profile Match Rate

- Target: 95%+
- Meaning: % of workers confirmed as a good role fit 30 days after arrival
- Timing: measured once, in month 1 per worker
- If below target: review selection stage and source-country partner

#### KPI 2. Time-to-Deploy

- Target: 56-84 days
- Meaning: average days from contract signature to worker functional on site
- Formula: arrival date minus contract signature date
- If above target: explain delay and define recovery plan

#### KPI 3. 90-Day Retention Rate

- Target: 96%+
- Meaning: % of workers still active at day 90
- Formula: active at day 90 / total arrived
- If below target: identify cause and fix within 30 days

#### KPI 4. Client NPS

- Target: 50+ NPS
- Meaning: quarterly recommendation score, reported monthly
- Trigger: if NPS < 30, urgent client meeting within 7 days

#### KPI 5. Worker Satisfaction Index

- Target: 4.2+/5
- Meaning: average worker satisfaction score from native-language survey
- Trigger: if below 4.0, intervene within 14 days

#### KPI 6. Major Incident Rate

- Target: 0
- Meaning: abandonments, serious conflicts, legal issues, unplanned repatriation
- Trigger: any incident requires a separate incident report within 48h

#### KPI 7. Contract Renewal Rate

- Target: 60%+
- Meaning: % of workers renewing for a second contract
- Timing: relevant from month 18 onward

#### KPI 8. Client Expansion Rate

- Target: 50%+ by month 12
- Meaning: increase in worker volume vs. initial contract
- Trigger: if still 0% by month 9+, schedule expansion discussion

## Worker Status Table

Track one row per active worker in the reported month:

- worker name
- arrival month
- contract month number
- attendance %
- WSI
- incidents
- short notes

If more than 20 workers are active, attach a separate tab/export.

## Incident Register

Only complete this section if incidents occurred in the reporting month.

Accepted incident types:

- abandonment
- conflict with colleagues
- conflict with supervisor
- medical issue
- legal issue
- unplanned repatriation
- unjustified absence
- other

If no incidents occurred, write explicitly:

`Zero incidente inregistrate in luna [MONTH/YEAR].`

## Next Month Plan

Minimum recurring actions:

- monthly individual check-in with all active workers
- monthly WSI survey
- permit expiry monitoring 90 days in advance

Add 2-3 month-specific actions with owner and deadline.

## PM Monthly Comment

Recommended structure:

- overall tone of the month
- what went well
- what risk appeared
- what Grow will improve next month
- a human observation about the team/client relationship

Rule: this section must be written fresh each month, not copied from a template.

## Pre-Send Checklist

- all 8 KPIs are completed with real values
- worker status table is updated
- incidents section is completed or explicitly marked zero
- next month plan has at least 3 concrete actions
- PM comment is written
- naming convention is respected: `GROW_[CLIENT]_[MONTH]_[YEAR].pdf`
- report is emailed to the main client contact
- report is archived with the client record in Airtable
- receipt is confirmed; if not, follow up within 48h

## Why This Matters

The monthly report is not admin work. It is the visible product of the partnership after delivery.

Good reporting helps Grow:

- retain accounts
- justify premium positioning
- surface risks early
- create expansion conversations
