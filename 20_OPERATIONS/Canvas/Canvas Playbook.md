# Canvas Playbook

## Canvas 1 - Value Map

Use this to visualize why Grow wins.

### Card Groups

- Sectors: Construction, Retail, Industry
- Core pain: labor shortage, turnover, unpredictability, lost output
- Grow promise: predictable teams, legal process, supported integration
- Proof: timelines, case studies, contract continuity
- Objections: cost, time, trust, housing
- Assets: scripts, SOPs, Dream 100 clusters

### Recommended Layout

- center: Grow value proposition
- left: target sectors
- right: pains and objections
- bottom: proof and scripts
- top: strategic thesis and positioning

## Canvas 2 - Recruitment Flow

Use this to visualize movement and bottlenecks.

### Lane Structure

1. Needs Analysis
2. Proposal and Collaboration Agreement
3. Sourcing and Selection
4. Candidate Confirmation
5. Legal Documentation and Compliance
6. Logistics and Arrival
7. Housing Orientation and Integration
8. Ongoing Support

### Visualization Rule

- green = healthy flow
- yellow = risk
- red = bottleneck
