# SOP - Logistics and Arrival

## Purpose

Coordinate travel and local arrival without handoff confusion.

## Inputs

- approved travel documents
- travel dates
- arrival destination

## Output

Workers arrive in Moldova and are transferred to the employer location.

## Risks

- missed travel coordination
- incomplete arrival planning
- employer unprepared on day one

## Automation Opportunity

Use one arrival checklist shared between Grow and the employer.
