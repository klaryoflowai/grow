# SOP - Needs Analysis

## Purpose

Clarify the exact workforce requirement before commercial or legal work begins.

## Inputs

- requested headcount
- role type
- skill level
- work schedule
- salary range
- housing conditions
- transport conditions

## Output

A clear hiring brief that Sales and Operations both understand.

## Risks

- vague job profile
- hidden accommodation gaps
- unrealistic timeline expectations

## Automation Opportunity

Standardize the intake questionnaire and qualification checklist.
