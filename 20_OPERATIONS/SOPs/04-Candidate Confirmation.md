# SOP - Candidate Confirmation

## Purpose

Secure final employer approval on the selected candidates.

## Inputs

- candidate shortlist
- employer feedback

## Output

Final approved candidate list for documentation.

## Risks

- delayed employer decisions
- late profile changes
- candidate drop-off after confirmation

## Automation Opportunity

Use one approval tracker with clear deadlines.
