# SOP - Legal Documentation and Compliance

## Purpose

Prepare the work permit, visa, and employment documentation in full legal compliance.

## Inputs

- approved candidates
- employer documents
- regulatory requirements

## Output

A complete legal file ready for movement to arrival.

## Risks

- missing documents
- compliance errors
- filing delays

## Automation Opportunity

Use stage-specific document checklists and a missing-documents dashboard.
