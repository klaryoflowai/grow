# SOP - Sourcing and Selection

## Purpose

Source and pre-screen candidates through trusted country partners.

## Inputs

- signed client agreement
- role profile
- target country mix

## Output

A shortlist of suitable candidates ready for review.

## Risks

- weak candidate filtering
- partner communication delays
- mismatch between profile and reality

## Automation Opportunity

Create a standard shortlist package with profile, experience, and interview status.
