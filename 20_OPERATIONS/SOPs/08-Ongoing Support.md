# SOP - Ongoing Support

## Purpose

Maintain continuity after deployment and resolve issues before they become account risk.

## Inputs

- active employer relationship
- worker feedback
- operational incidents

## Output

Stable account performance with fewer surprises and lower churn risk.

## Risks

- unresolved issues
- poor communication loops
- preventable attrition

## Automation Opportunity

Track issues by account, owner, severity, and time-to-resolution.
