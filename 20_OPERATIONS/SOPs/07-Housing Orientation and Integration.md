# SOP - Housing Orientation and Integration

## Purpose

Stabilize the first days after arrival so workers adapt quickly and employers feel in control.

## Inputs

- housing readiness
- orientation materials
- workplace rules

## Output

Workers are housed, oriented, and integrated into the work environment.

## Risks

- poor living conditions
- weak communication
- early dissatisfaction

## Automation Opportunity

Create a first-week integration checklist and issue log.
