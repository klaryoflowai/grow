# SOP - Proposal and Collaboration Agreement

## Purpose

Convert the validated need into a clear commercial proposal and signed collaboration framework.

## Inputs

- approved hiring brief
- commercial assumptions
- legal responsibilities

## Output

Signed collaboration agreement with aligned expectations on scope, timing, and responsibilities.

## Risks

- unclear scope
- responsibility gaps
- discounting without strategic fit

## Automation Opportunity

Use one proposal template and one approval path for exceptions.
