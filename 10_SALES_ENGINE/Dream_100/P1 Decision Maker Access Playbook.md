# P1 Decision Maker Access Playbook

Date: 2026-05-24  
Related: [[P1 Top 20 Strategic Accounts]]

## Principle

For large Moldovan accounts, generic email is the weakest path. The strongest path is:

```text
warm route + operational satellite + proof asset + small next step
```

## Access Routes

| Route | Use when | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| Warm institutional route | account is corporate, foreign-owned or association-active | AmCham, FIA, EBA, bilateral chambers, Invest Moldova, ZEL administration |
| Operational satellite | CEO is hard to reach but operational pain is visible | Plant Manager, Production Director, COO, Supply Chain Director, Logistics Director |
| Supplier route | vendors already see the bottleneck | equipment suppliers, WMS/ERP vendors, 3PL, transporters, H&S consultants, auditors, banks |
| Proof-first route | fear and trust are the blockers | existing client visit, client reference call, housing/integration video, cost calculator, replacement guarantee |

## 7-Step Account Access Flow

1. Check Airtable for existing record and history.
2. Verify current decision makers; mark names as `to_verify` until confirmed.
3. Choose 3 paths: warm, operational and fallback cold.
4. Write a Challenger first touch under 120 words.
5. Attach or prepare one proof asset relevant to the account.
6. Ask for a 15-minute diagnostic, not a long presentation.
7. Log every action in Airtable and AI context.

## Message For CEO / Owner

```text
Buna ziua, [Nume]. Lucrez cu Grow pe stabilizarea fortei de munca pentru companii operationale din Moldova.

Motivul pentru care va scriu nu este sa va propun "inca o agentie", ci sa discutam un model prin care companiile reduc cautarea permanenta de inlocuitori pentru pozitii grele/necalificate si securizeaza echipe pe 24 luni.

Cred ca pentru [Companie] subiectul poate fi relevant mai ales pe [durere sectoriala]. Va propun 15 minute ca sa vedem daca exista un caz real sau nu.
```

## Message For Operational Director

```text
Buna ziua, [Nume]. Am o intrebare foarte concreta: unde va creeaza cea mai mare presiune instabilitatea oamenilor - linie, depozit, schimb de noapte sau sezon?

Noi lucram pe un model de echipe internationale stabile pe 24 luni, cu integrare si inlocuire controlata. Nu vreau sa va trimit o oferta generica. Daca imi spuneti unde apare blocajul, va pot arata daca modelul are sens pentru voi.
```

## Message For HR

```text
Buna ziua, [Nume]. Presupun ca problema nu este doar recrutarea initiala, ci faptul ca oamenii locali nu raman suficient pe rolurile grele.

Grow poate deveni o a doua optiune comparabila langa solutia curenta: echipe internationale, proces legal, cazare/integrare gestionate si garantie de inlocuire. Nu cerem sa schimbati nimic acum; merita doar sa aveti alternativa pregatita daca apare un gol.
```

## 10-Day Sprint

| Day | Action | Output |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | select 5 P1 accounts | shortlist with reason |
| 2 | check Airtable and public data | status and gaps |
| 3 | map 3 routes per account | warm, ops, cold |
| 4 | prepare proof asset by sector | link or document |
| 5 | send first intro requests | 5 logged actions |
| 6-7 | contact operational satellites | attempted conversations |
| 8 | follow up and adjust message | objections captured |
| 9 | book diagnostics | dated next steps |
| 10 | AI/BOD review | keep, change, stop decisions |
