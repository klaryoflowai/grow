# Grow Moldova - Corporate Presentation

## Purpose

This note captures the structure and core messages from the Grow Moldova corporate presentation asset.

Use it when preparing:

- client presentations
- board-style summary decks
- partner intros
- investor or strategic partner conversations

## Presentation Spine

### 1. Cover

Core message:

- sustainable solution for labor shortage
- international recruitment and integration
- source countries: Nepal, Sri Lanka, Bangladesh

### 2. Grow In Numbers

Key figures from the asset:

- 1000+ workers integrated or in process of arrival
- 3 source countries
- 10+ active partner companies
- about 12 weeks from contract to arrival

### 3. Market Context

Main thesis:

- Moldova faces a structural labor shortage
- the damage is operational, reputational, and financial
- emergency hiring is unstable; businesses need predictability

### 4. Who Grow Is

Positioning:

- international recruitment specialist
- full management from sourcing to integration
- disciplined workforce profile
- 24-month stability mindset

### 5. Industries Served

Named sectors:

- construction
- agriculture
- viticulture
- HoReCa
- factories and logistics

### 6. Active Clients

Named examples in the asset:

- Linella
- Asconi Winery
- Golban Trade

Use this section as social proof, not just branding.

### 7. Worker Profile

Core traits:

- physical resilience
- discipline
- stability
- basic English at arrival
- functional Romanian for Nepali workers in 3-4 months

### 8. What Grow Handles

Operational scope:

- recruitment
- legal documentation
- logistics
- integration and support

This is the key slide for reducing client anxiety.

### 9. Investment and Timeline

Positioning from the asset:

- state fees are standardized
- Grow fee depends on project complexity and volume
- timeline is predictable enough to plan against

Use carefully: this is a framing slide, not a substitute for a real offer.

## Reusable Talk Track

- problem: labor shortage is structural, not temporary
- consequence: delay, churn, missed output, management distraction
- solution: Grow handles sourcing, legality, logistics, and integration
- proof: existing clients, delivered workers, structured process
- outcome: predictable workforce continuity

## Best Use Cases

- first serious presentation after qualification
- proposal support meeting
- meetings with larger accounts that need multiple workers
- meetings where the client needs a strategic, not tactical, explanation
