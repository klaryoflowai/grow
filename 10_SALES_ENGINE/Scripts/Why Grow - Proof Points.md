# Why Grow - Proof Points

## Purpose

This note turns the "De ce Grow?" asset into reusable sales proof points for calls, meetings, proposals, and follow-up emails.

## Core Proof Bar

Use these as fast credibility anchors:

- 800+ workers delivered
- 0 major incidents in the first year
- 5+ active clients
- about 10 weeks delivery

## The 10 Reasons

### 1. Zero major incidents

Claim: zero major incidents in the first year across 800+ integrated workers.

Use when:

- the client fears operational risk
- the client compares Grow with lower-discipline agencies

### 2. SEBN-level compliance

Claim: Grow works with SEBN / Sumitomo Electric and has passed multinational-grade compliance expectations.

Use when:

- the client asks whether Grow can handle serious audit or compliance standards

### 3. 7-step selection system

Claim: technical, psychometric, medical, background, interview, orientation, final approval.

Use when:

- the client worries about worker quality
- the client asks what makes Grow different from "people brokers"

### 4. Romanian adaptation

Claim: Nepali workers reach functional Romanian in 3-4 months.

Use when:

- the client worries about communication on site

### 5. Free replacement in first 90 days

Claim: replacement with no extra Grow commission and no new state taxes if the worker does not match the profile.

Use when:

- the client pushes back on risk
- the client asks "what if it does not work?"

### 6. Monthly report with 8 KPIs

Claim: Grow stays involved after deployment with structured monthly reporting.

Use when:

- the client thinks agencies disappear after delivery
- the client wants accountability

### 7. Full-service delivery

Claim: visas, IGM, transport, housing, cultural integration, 24-month support.

Use when:

- the client is overwhelmed by bureaucracy

### 8. 24-month contracts

Claim: stability beats constant churn and retraining.

Use when:

- the client has high turnover
- the client thinks local stopgap hiring is cheaper

### 9. Verifiable portfolio

Named references in the asset:

- Linella
- SEBN
- Golban Trade
- Sigma Venmer
- Asconi Winery

Use when:

- the client asks for social proof
- the client says "everyone claims they can do this"

### 10. Service, not transaction

Claim: others deliver people; Grow delivers 24-month operational continuity.

Use when:

- you want to frame Grow as a strategic partner, not a commodity supplier

## Short Positioning Lines

- "Nu livram oameni; livram continuitate operationala."
- "Riscul de executie nu trebuie sa stea la client."
- "Daca trecem de auditul unei multinationale, putem sustine si standardele dvs."
- "Un om stabil 2 ani valoreaza mai mult decat 6 rotatii improvizate."

## Best Places To Use This Note

- first meeting decks
- "Why us" proposal section
- objection handling
- follow-up emails after discovery
- verbal proof during pricing conversations
