# Strategy Map

## Mission

Transform Moldova's labor shortage into a predictable assembly line for staffing through international workforce delivery from Nepal, Sri Lanka, and Bangladesh.

## Role Thesis

As Director of Sales, your job is not only to close deals. Your job is to design a system that:

- targets high-volume accounts
- qualifies for scalability
- creates predictable pipeline movement
- protects Operations from low-quality deals

## Core Strategic Questions

- Which sectors can absorb teams repeatedly?
- Which clients are scalable versus distracting?
- Where does revenue stall: targeting, meetings, proposals, or operations?
- Which objections appear most often and how do we standardize the response?
