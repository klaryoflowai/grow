Date: 2026-05-24

## Unified Operating Logic

Grow combines five frameworks:

| Framework | Operating role |
|---|---|
| New Sales Simplified | hunting discipline and Hit-List focus |
| Challenger Sale | reframe and executive-level insight |
| Never Split the Difference | negotiation, fears and trust |
| Theory of Constraints | protect delivery capacity and avoid false promises |
| Scaling Up | weekly rhythm, KPIs and scalable execution |

## Core Positioning

```text
Grow sells operational continuity through legal, stable international workforce teams.
```

## Practical Rules

- Prospecting starts from P1 accounts, not random outreach.
- Every message must connect to an operational pain.
- Every meeting must reveal volume, timing, decision process, conditions and fear.
- Every promise must be checked against Operations capacity.
- Every important discussion must be logged in AI/vault/CRM.
- Every active lead must have a next step with a date.

## Voss Labels For Client Fears

- "Se pare ca principala ingrijorare nu este comisionul, ci daca oamenii vor ramane si vor munci stabil."
- "Se simte ca si cum vreti sa evitati inca un ciclu de cautare permanenta si inlocuiri."
- "Arata ca si cum aveti nevoie de dovada concreta, nu doar de o promisiune comerciala."

## Goldratt Guardrail

Sales should never create demand that Operations cannot legally and logistically deliver.

Before pushing volume, check:

- sourcing capacity;
- documentation;
- visa/permit timeline;
- housing;
- onboarding;
- replacement process.
