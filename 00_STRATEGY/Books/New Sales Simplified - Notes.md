# New Sales Simplified - Notes

## Why This Matters

Use this note to capture prospecting ideas, targeting rules, and high-output sales discipline.

## Capture Prompts

- What does our Dream 100 universe look like?
- Which outreach messaging earns meetings fastest?
- Which assumptions are blocking prospecting velocity?
- What does a good first call qualify or disqualify?

## Translation Into This Vault

- Dream 100 stays separate from active opportunities.
- Meetings create action items and next step dates.
- Scripts and objection handling are kept in one place.
