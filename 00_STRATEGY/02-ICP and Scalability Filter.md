# ICP and Scalability Filter

## Ideal Customer Profile

Priority sectors for Grow:

- Construction
- Retail
- Industry and manufacturing

Secondary sectors:

- Hospitality
- Agriculture
- Logistics

## Scalability Filter

Ask these before pushing any deal forward:

1. Can this client absorb teams, not just one worker?
2. Is the demand recurring or seasonal but predictable?
3. Does the client have operational maturity for housing, transport, and onboarding?
4. Will this account create a reusable playbook?
5. Will this account strengthen our scorecard or distort it?

## Priority Guide

- `priority: 1` = strategic, high-volume, recurring, executive attention
- `priority: 2` = solid fit, worth pursuing, but not a flagship account
- `priority: 3` = low leverage, only if process cost is minimal
